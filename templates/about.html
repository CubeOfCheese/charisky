About

Charisky was developed on the idea of its user's taking a risk with their money and even if they lose, the money would go towards a good cause. A user first bets how long they can be productive with a dollar or two, and if manage to do so in the betted time, their money is returned. Otherwise, the money is donated to a selected charity!

We are 2018 Sachacks team made of first time hackathon attenders. We want you to be productive when you work, and when you're not, at least you're donating to charity.
