Goals

We want you to be productive, and when you're not, at least you're donating to charity. Charisky was developed on the idea of its user's taking a risk with their money and even if they lose, the money would go towards a good cause. A user first bets how long they can be productive with a dollar or two, and if manage to do so in the betted time, their money is returned. Otherwise, the money is donated to a selected charity!
